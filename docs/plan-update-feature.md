# Plan Update Feature - User Guide

## Overview

The Plan Update feature allows users to purchase eligible plans directly from the CurrIA dashboard without leaving the authenticated area. When a plan purchase is allowed, remaining credits are carried over automatically so unused credits are preserved.

## How to Access

1. Navigate to **Settings** (`/settings`) from the dashboard
2. Look for the **"Alterar plano"** button in the top section
3. Click to open the Plan Update dialog

## Limitations

### Monthly Plan Changes
- If you're on a **monthly plan** (Mensal or Pro), you cannot directly switch to another **monthly plan** from this dialog
- **Reason**: Asaas treats each subscription separately; switching would create two active subscriptions
- **Solution**: Cancel your current monthly plan first, then purchase the new plan

### One-Time Plans
- You can purchase one-time plans (Unitário) while on a monthly plan
- This adds credits without affecting your subscription

## Credit Carryover Behavior

### When You Purchase Another Eligible Plan

When you purchase another eligible plan, your remaining credits from the previous balance are **preserved and added** to the new plan credits.

**Example 1: Upgrade**
- You're on Unitário plan with 1 credit remaining
- You upgrade to Mensal (20 credits)
- Result: 1 + 20 = **21 total credits**

**Example 2: One-Time Top-Up While Monthly Is Active**
- You're on Mensal plan with 5 credits remaining
- You purchase Unitario (3 credits)
- Result: 5 + 3 = **8 total credits**

### When Your Monthly Plan Renews

When your monthly subscription automatically renews, credits are **replaced** (not added). This prevents unlimited credit accumulation.

**Example: Monthly Renewal**
- You're on Mensal plan with 6 credits remaining from last month
- Your monthly subscription renews
- Result: Exactly **20 credits** (previous balance is replaced)

This means unused credits from the previous month are not carried over to the next renewal. Plan your usage accordingly!

## Making a Plan Purchase

1. Open the Plan Update dialog from Settings
2. Browse the available plans
3. Each plan card shows:
   - Plan name and description
   - Monthly or one-time price
   - Number of credits included
   - Estimated total credits after the purchase when applicable
4. Click **"Selecionar"** to proceed when the option is available
5. You'll be redirected to the Asaas checkout page
6. Complete the payment process
7. After successful payment, your credits will be updated automatically

## What Happens to Your Data

- Your session history is preserved
- Your generated resumes remain available
- Your account information stays the same
- Only your plan and available credits change

## Subscription Management

### Monthly Plans (Mensal and Pro)
- Automatically renew every 30 days
- Renewal date shown in your Asaas account
- Can be canceled anytime (see below)

### One-Time Plans (Unitário)
- No automatic renewal
- Single purchase of 3 credits
- Can purchase again anytime

### Canceling Your Subscription

To cancel a monthly subscription:
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to Settings
3. Contact support or access your Asaas billing dashboard
4. Cancel the recurring subscription

**Note**: Canceling does NOT revoke already-earned credits. Any unused credits will remain in your account for future use.

## Billing & Payment

- All prices are in Brazilian Reais (R$)
- Payments are processed through Asaas
- Payment methods accepted vary by region
- Invoices are sent to your registered email
- Payment failures will halt the purchase flow - you'll be notified

- **Credit updates**: Allow up to 2 minutes for credits to update after successful payment

## FAQ

**Q: What if I purchase a higher plan but haven't used all credits from my current plan?**
A: Your unused credits are automatically added to your new plan's credits.

**Q: Can I change plans multiple times?**
A: You can make repeated purchases, but you cannot contract a second monthly plan while another monthly subscription is still active. If you want to move from Mensal to Pro or from Pro to Mensal, cancel the active monthly subscription first and then purchase the new one.

**Q: What happens to my credits if I cancel my subscription?**
A: Your unused credits remain in your account and can be used anytime you re-subscribe or purchase a new plan.

**Q: Are there any hidden fees?**
A: No. The price shown is the total amount you'll pay. No additional fees or taxes are added at checkout.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Refund policies vary based on your payment method and circumstances. Please contact support for refund requests.
