- Phase 37 established the shared `JobStatusSnapshot`, repository, idempotent create semantics, and source-of-truth helpers the later phases consume.

- Phase 39 connected that dispatch path to in-process durable processors and fenced runtime writes.
- Phase 40 exposed the resulting job state through `/api/jobs/[jobId]`, `/api/session/[id]`, `/api/file/[sessionId]`, dashboard polling, and structured logs.
