<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| billing audit tables -> user-facing read model | Internal reservation details cross into authenticated product copy and must stay user-scoped |
| billing audit tables -> operator anomaly summary | Alerting reads must remain read-only and not mutate financial state |
| shared DTOs -> later UI/API plans | Contract drift here would cascade into user and operator surfaces |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-45-01 | I | user-scoped ledger reads | mitigate | Filter all history helpers by `user_id` and keep DTOs free of cross-user raw identifiers not needed by the UI. |
| T-45-02 | T | anomaly summarizer | mitigate | Keep alert helpers read-only and derive from existing reservation and ledger tables without writing reconciliation state. |
| T-45-03 | R | exported history labels | mitigate | Map raw ledger enums into explicit product-safe labels so audit events remain attributable and understandable. |
| T-45-04 | D | alert noise | mitigate | Encode age and volume thresholds in the contract so transient reconciliation states do not page operators immediately. |
</threat_model>
